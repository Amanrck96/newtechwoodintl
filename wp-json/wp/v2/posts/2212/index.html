Benefits of a Composite Raised Garden Bed or Planter<\/h2>\n

Composite raised garden beds and planters can help\u00a0solve some of the most common gardening problems<\/a>, from the backbreaking work of digging a bed to the limited lawn space that can come with some homes or business locations.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

A raised garden bed requires no digging, and you can customize it to any height or size. Plus, they help promote proper plant growth and look great on porches, decks or balconies. With the help of a raised bed made from composite lumber, you\u2019ll be able to:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Prepare with ease:\u00a0<\/strong>Raised beds are relatively simple to construct, especially with a kit. You can customize it to the size you need and, if you\u2019d like to avoid hours of kneeling or bending when planting, layer the bed walls higher to create taller boxes.<\/li>\n
  • Control soil pH:<\/strong>\u00a0Depending on what plants you choose, the soil needs to have a specific composition, depth and pH balance. While it is difficult or sometimes impossible to convert natural soil to the pH level your plants need, raised beds let you layer the correct materials and create the best environment possible.<\/li>\n
  • Provide water drainage:\u00a0<\/strong>Too much water can cause plants to contract bacterial and fungal diseases. Raised garden beds reduce the chances of flooding or overwatering, making them especially useful for areas with heavy rains.<\/li>\n
  • Aerate soil:<\/strong>\u00a0The soil in your yard is continually being compacted by weather, objects and time, which isn\u2019t ideal for gardening purposes. In a composite garden planter, you layer soil and other materials loosely, providing better air circulation and allowing the roots to develop.<\/li>\n
  • Avoid weed growth:\u00a0<\/strong>Beds not separated from grass can take on weeds that grow up through the soil, which could result in your plants dying. Raised beds avoid this problem, being completely separate and installable anywhere.<\/li>\n
  • Start gardening earlier:<\/strong>\u00a0With gardening, you have to wait for the right ground temperature or else risk your plants not growing. With the loose soil and disconnect from ground temperatures, you can start working in raised garden beds sooner, as they aren\u2019t as susceptible to frost and thaw out quicker.<\/li>\n
  • Garden anywhere:<\/strong>\u00a0Raised planters allow you to grow a garden wherever you like, even if you don\u2019t have a lawn. With enough sun and water, even those living in apartments or businesses in dense cities can enjoy flowers and fresh vegetables.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    With so many upsides, composite garden beds are an excellent option for landscapers and gardeners.<\/p>\n

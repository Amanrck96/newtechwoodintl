UltraShield Technology

In 2010, NewTechWood started developing UltraShield®: the industry’s top – notch, durable, fade – and scratch – resistant composite decking.

360-Degree Capping

UltraShield all-weather decking fuses high density polyethylene (HDPE) plastic & wood fibers with a full-encapsulating polymer shell, delivering scratch, stain, and fade resistance.

Co-Extruded Cap
Composite Core

Co-Extruded Cap

Capped wood plastic composite (WPC) tech: advanced shield fully wraps all four sides of the core. High-temp co- xtrusion fuses shield & core (no harmful adhesives/chemicals), ensuring eco-friendly structural integrity.

Composite Core

Core blends recycled materials, dense hardwood/softwood fibers — boosting strength & durability. 100% recyclable, balancing performance with sustainability.

UltraShield® is embossed with a beautiful natural wood grain that has NewTechWood’s distinct Naturale surface treatment that will ensure your feet never want to leave your deck.

Features & Benefits

NewTechWood’s cutting-edge manufacturing facility is where innovation meets precision. Witness the seamless flow of raw materials through advanced machinery, showcasing the transformative journey from blending to extrusion. Our dedicated team ensures strict quality control measures resulting in a product that is built to last. Experience the perfect fusion of nature, technology, and craftsmanship in every product that leaves the facility.

Stain Resistance

Wine, coffee, soy sauce, oil — liquids won’t soak in. Spills wipe off easily, making cleaning simple and upkeep low.

25 Year Warranty

Supported by a 25-year limited warranty for lasting peace of mind.

Environmentally Friendly

High-temp co-extrusion (no harmful adhesives/chemicals) ensures eco-safe structure. 100% recyclable.

No Painting Needed

Multi-tone colors are part of the shield — no painting, sanding, or oiling required. Plus, it’s our most scratch-resistant, appealing design.

Mold/Mildew Resistance

The shield blocks mold/mildew from penetrating the core. Surface growth (from standing water) cleans off easily with a hose or broom.

UV Protection

Unique shield formula locks in color far longer than standard composites. Testing shows minimal fading even after years.

Naturale

Offering the most natural looking composite in the industry with an outstanding range of 16 colors.

Introducing Naturale – our line of composite product that features a unique brushing process to provide an authentic, natural look and feel. Our proprietary brushing technique enhances the texture and grain of the material, creating a rich, organic appearance that mimics the beauty of natural wood.

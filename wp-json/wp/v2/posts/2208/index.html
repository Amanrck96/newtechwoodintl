Composite wood decking<\/a>\u00a0is made from a variety of plastics, resins, acrylics, and recycled waste, has been around for several decades as the industry looked for ways to re-use all that plastic we produce each year \u2013 the stuff that\u2019s packing our landfills beyond capacity.<\/p>\n

These early attempts at developing a natural-looking and durable composite material produced less than stellar results. The end product didn\u2019t look like real wood. It didn\u2019t feel like real wood. It could become slick when wet, causing slips and falls. Product quality was all over the place, and the consumer wasn\u2019t impressed.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

That changed in 2010 when NewTechWood introduced UltraShield\u00ae to create the most natural, durable, eye-catching wood composite that\u2019s low on maintenance, and high on eye appeal. NewTechWood, with UltraShield, looks like real wood, and it\u2019s engineered to address the problems when real wood and real weather meet up around your home or commercial venue.<\/p>\n

What IS UltraShield from NewTechWood?<\/h2>\n

It\u2019s the advancement that actually makes engineered wood composite better than organic wood \u2013 engineered for excellence.<\/p>\n

Our researchers conducted countless hours of field testing to create a wood composite that looks great, feels like the real deal, and delivers numerous advantages over pressure-treated (PT) lumber commonly used in deck construction.<\/p>\n

UltraShield is unparalleled in strength, beauty, toughness, stability, and long-lasting good looks. In fact, NewTechWood with UltraShield composite decking is warranted for 25 years, with a five-year transferable warranty to a new owner \u2013 a great selling point if you plan to sell.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Our decking,\u00a0siding<\/a>,\u00a0composite floor tiles<\/a>, and accessories resist scratching, staining, and fading \u2013 even under heavy, family use. It\u2019ll stand up to chlorinated pool water without staining, and it\u2019ll last for years in builds near salt water, which is always tough on natural wood.<\/p>\n

UltraShield delivers the look and barefoot feel of real wood. No slick surfaces to create a slippery danger of falling. NewTechWood infuses UltraShield composite with wood fibers to create the look and feel of real wood. Get down for a closer look at the texture and eye appeal only UltraShield from NewTechWood delivers.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Why UltraShield is just better.<\/h2>\n

It\u2019s better because it\u2019s a capped wood-plastic product wrapped in an advanced, premium shield encasing ALL four sides of the inner core.<\/p>\n

This protective shield and core are fabricated together under high temperatures to bond, so UltraShield products don\u2019t need adhesives or toxic chemicals that eventually work their way into your living environment. Each UltraShield product is engineered for long-term excellence, and a natural living experience for you, your family, and friends.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Capping 360 degrees delivers complete protection \u2013 even in the grooved stock. No mold, no mildew, no allergens. Just easy maintenance that lasts for decades.<\/p>\n

Another reason to choose NewTechWood? The core is created using high-density, recycled plastics and wood fibers that would, otherwise, crowd our already over-crowded landfills. Using\u00a0recycled materials<\/a>\u00a0greatly shrinks your carbon footprint, while delivering the good looks and natural feel of wood-hued composite.<\/p>\n

UltraShield is engineered to last \u2013 and that is backed up with a 25-year transferable limited warranty.<\/p>\n

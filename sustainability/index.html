Sustainability

We were green before green.

85%

Recycled Material Ratio

NewTechWood uses over 85% recycled resources in its WPC products, including waste plastics and agricultural residues.

14,000T

Recycled Plastic Usage (2025)

NewTechWood utilized 14,000 tons of waste plastic in production in 2025.

100%

Production Waste Recycling

NewTechWood achieves 100% recycling of production scraps and defective products.

Closed Loop Process

NewTechWood uses selected recycled high-density PE (post-consumer), combined with recycled wood fiber from furniture cut-offs, shavings, sawdust etc. as feedstocks, its products are 100% recyclable after their life spans.

Recycling

NewTechWood diverts over 13,000 tons of plastic waste from landfills and incineration every year. This reduces the amount of plastic that would otherwise contribute to pollution and environmental degradation.

Conservation

NewTechWood offers a sustainable alternative to traditional wood, made from recycled wood waste—like sawdust, chips, cutoffs—by products of sustainable forestry and wood manufacturing. Using 100% recycled wood reduces logging pressure, saving nearly 900,000 trees from being cut down.

Lower Environmental Impact

NewTechWood’s requires less energy and emits fewer greenhouse gases compared to traditional wood products. This lower environmental impact helps to mitigate climate change and reduces the overall ecological footprint associated with manufacturing and construction activities.

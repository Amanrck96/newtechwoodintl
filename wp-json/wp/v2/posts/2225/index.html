Why Is NewTechWood Composite the Best Choice for Your Family Home?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

There are so many types of decks in the market today. But what commonly gets people attracted to their choice of composite deck includes durability, alluring appeal, low maintenance, and cost-effectiveness.<\/p>\n

Here\u2019s why NewTechWood composite decking is family-friendly and loved by a lot of homeowners all around the world:<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

1. Stain Resistance<\/strong><\/h4>\n

If you have kids at home, then you know how chaotic they can be. Crumbs fall while they eat, drinks get knocked over, toys are left lying all over the house, and they draw on literally anything. With the right products, you can try to clean the stains off.<\/p>\n

However, wood decks are not stain-resistant and tend to absorb liquids (jelly, grease or juice) quickly. These stains are usually very difficult to remove. PVC decks are also easily stained with bug spray and suntan lotion.<\/p>\n

On the other hand, capped composite decking from NewTechWood is stain-resistant and can easily be cleaned with just warm water and soap. And even after cleaning it thousands of times, it\u2019ll not lose its luster \u2013 it\u2019ll still look new all through the years.<\/p>\n

So you never have to worry about your kids or other guests spilling drinks on your deck.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

2. Safety at Its Best<\/strong><\/h4>\n

Safety at home should always be your number one priority, especially if you have children. One of the best preventative methods is installing a composite deck.<\/p>\n

Unlike other decking materials like wood \u2013 which wears down when exposed to weather or when used constantly \u2013 composite is tough and sturdy. It is not susceptible to rot, mold, and mildew which may flare up allergies in your home.<\/p>\n

Minor damages such as splinters on your deck can be quite dangerous to you and your family. Our composite decking does not splinter so you don\u2019t have to worry about such accidents.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

And if your kids love playing on the deck, you might consider installing NewTechWood deck railings. They\u2019ll not only enhance your deck\u2019s safety but will also last a very long time. Additionally, they don\u2019t use iron nails during installation. If driven wrongly, these nails can be very risky.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

3. Beautiful Colors and Styles to Choose From<\/strong><\/h4>\n

Although longevity and sturdiness are essential aspects of a deck to every family, we know you still want something visually attractive.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

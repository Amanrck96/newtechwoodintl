Recently, NewTechWood Quick Deck deck tiles were featured as part of the Arnold Palmer Experience at the Arnold Palmer Invitational Golf Tournament at Bay Hill. Our Icelandic Smoke White deck tiles were used in the entrance and the Peruvian Teak deck tiles were used in the exhibit itself. We are extremely grateful to be included in this notable event.<\/p>\n
